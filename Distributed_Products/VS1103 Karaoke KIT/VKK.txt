Features

·                  General midi I / SP midi synthesizer

·                  Takes midi stream from standard midi connector

·                  Reverb processing

·                  Uses VS10XX Proto Board with added board for Midi connector

Description

VS1103 Karaoke kit is complete example of portable karaoke player. It can play WAV and MIDI files and mix the decoded output to microphone signal.

The mixed signal is available at the analog output that can be connected to headphones or amplifier.

The output signal of the mixer can also be recorded as WAV file.

The KIT comes with microphone and two SD cards, one as source of the WAV or MIDI input, the other as target of the recorded output.
